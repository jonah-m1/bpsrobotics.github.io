Team Registration

There are a few steps that are needed to register a student on our team. This page will take you through each step. A general Process overview can be found here. Theses forms should be filled out with a parent or guardian and student together.

Here is a competition schedule and agenda for the upcoming build season. Check back on these often as they may change as the season progresses.

  • Competition Schedule
  • Agenda

  1. The Team Handbook

    2. Read the Team Handbook which can be found here or on the right-hand side of the 1510 website. After doing so print out and fill out this agreement acknowledging you read and understand the handbook. If you ever have a question during the build season or competition, do not hesitate to look it up in the handbook.

  2. Register for FIRST STIMS

    3. Standing for: Student Team Information Member System. This is for the global competition that we compete in, instructions are provided on the site found here. It will ask you several questions, when it ask what program you are a part of answer: FRC and when it asks what team answer: 1510 The Wildcats. After you finish this section, it will send you and the coaches an email saying that you have completed this part of the registration. If you have any questions this document will answer them.

  3. BSD Forms and Agreements

    4. All of the below listed forms need to be printed out and filled out. After each one has been completed, they must be turned into a coach in a TIDY packet or folder.

    1. Form Instructions
    2. FIRST Student Process Overview
    3. Participant Code of Conduct
    4. Release of Liability & Hold Harmless
    5. Form B - Transportation and Release
    6. Form C - Private Auto Driver & Passenger Release
    7. Form E - Student Contract Overnight or out of State
    8. Form F - Health History Overnight Out Of State (OOS)

    After all the forms have been filled out, there should be six forms filled out.
