Team Registration

There are a few steps that are needed to register a student on our team. This page will take you through each step. A general Process overview can be found here. Theses forms should be filled out with a parent or guardian and student together.

  1. The Team Handbook

    2. Read the Team Handbook which can be found here or on the right-hand side of the 1510 website. After doing so print out and fill out this agreement acknowledging you read and understand the handbook. If you ever have a question during the build season or competition, do not hesitate to look it up in the handbook.

  2. Register for FIRST STIMS

    3. Standing for: Student Team Information Member System. This is for the global competition that we compete in, instructions are provided on the site found here. It will ask you several questions, when it ask what program you are a part of answer: FRC and when it asks what team answer: 1510 The Wildcats. After you finish this section, it will send you and the coaches an email saying that you have completed this part of the registration.

    If you have any questions this document should answer them. If any questions remain, talk to the coach or a mentor.

  3. BSD Forms and Agreements

    4. Instructions and Information

    • Form Instructions
    • FIRST Student Process Overview

    All of the below listed forms need to be printed out and filled out. After each one has been completed, they must be turned into the coach in a TIDY packet or folder.

    1. Participant Code of Conduct
    2. Release of Liability & Hold Harmless
    3. Form B - Transportation and Release
    4. Form C - Private Auto Driver & Passenger Release
    5. Form E - Student Contract Overnight or out of State
    6. Form F - Health History Overnight Out Of State (OOS)

    After all the forms have been filled out, there should be six forms filled out, and turned into the coach or head mentor.

    For students in need of financial aid, fill out form form and talk with the coach.
